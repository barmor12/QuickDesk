# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers who work with local AI coding tools, terminals, simulators, browser tabs, and desktop workflows, and want a fast way to control their Mac from iPhone and Apple Watch.

## Product Purpose

QuickDesk is a local-first command center for running trusted Mac workflows and approving Claude Code or Codex permission prompts from iPhone and Apple Watch. Success means fewer context switches, faster approvals, and reliable remote control across Wi-Fi and Tailscale.

## Brand Personality

Calm, capable, precise. The product should feel like a professional developer console that happens to fit in your hand and on your wrist.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-style landing page composition, oversized decorative cards, toy-like productivity UI, vague AI assistant copy, and single-hue dashboard monotony.

## Design Principles

- Put live operational state first: connection, pending approvals, and agent health should be visible without hunting.
- Prefer one-tap developer workflows over configuration-heavy screens.
- Keep risky actions explicit and calm, with clear labels and confirmation where needed.
- Make remote use feel normal: Tailscale, local network, and watch sync should be presented as one coherent connection story.
- Use compact, scan-friendly layouts that hold up during repeated daily use.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target readable contrast, 44-point touch targets, clear icon labels, reduced reliance on color-only status, and layouts that remain usable on compact iPhone and Apple Watch screens.
